North Idaho College

I had to opportunity to play soccer for two seasons with the NIC womens soccer team! It was an amazing experience that lead me to make many meaningful connections and experiences that I will carry with me for the rest of my life.

Zimbabwe, Africa

I got to spend a year and a half living in Zimbabwe, Harare, Africa! I was able to teach the gospel of Jesus Christ and immerse myself in one of the most beautiful cultures I have ever seen. It was an amazing experience.

Unforum 2024

I was put in charge of organizing an event for the BYU student body called Unforum! It was meant to be an end of the year pep rally that reflected on all that we accomplished this last year as a student body! We had nearly 4,000 people attend and had performances from the BYU Dunk team, the BYU International Folk Dance Ensemble, the BYU Rhythm and Soul Collective, our most recent BYU Battle of the Bands Champion, the BYU Vocalpoint and Noteworthy singers, and our very own BYU Divine Comedy team!

Take a moment to enjoy it!
